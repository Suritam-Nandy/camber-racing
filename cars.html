OUR CARS

CR 18

2018

CR 18 was the challenger developed by the team for the 2018 season. It was a robust improvement from its predecessor in terms of power output and aerodynamic grip. This was the team's first turbocharged car and also featured a fully in house manufactured Aero kit. It was the fastest car at Supra 2018, clocking 5.4 seconds in the acceleration event. Vehicle dynamics were optimized from CR16 to include a wider range of permorance to choose from its setups. It set a benchmark for future designs the team has worked on.


ACHIEVEMENTS:


    Supra SAE India 2018
  • 1st Place Acceleration

  • Custom Designed Steering Gearbox with 220 deg lock to lock
