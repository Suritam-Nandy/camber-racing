Camber Racing is the official Formula Student Combustion team of SRM IST, Chennai. It all started over at a coffee table in 2009 when a group of young automotive enthusiasts decided to turn their dreams into reality and unleash their passion. After a proposing a well -versed plan to the HOD, the team made its first prototype “CR 800”, engineered with passion and excellence. This, was the birth of Camber Racing. Since its evolution in 2009, the team has participated in various Formula Student events namely Formula Bharat, SUPRA SAE and Formula Student Germany. It comprises a passionate team of Undergraduate students from various disciplines, who apply their collective intelligence to engineer the ultimate racing machine. The team has added 26 awards and 2 national championships to the cabinet building 9 cars and completing 10 glorious years.

Our Motto

‘Building People not just cars’. Camber Racing believes in not only achieving excellence in designing and engineering a race car but also in developing skilled, competent individuals and cordial relations with the college fraternity, sponsors and FSAE student community.

Our Vision

To pursue excellence in designing and building one of the best cars in the country and compete in national and international FSAE events.
